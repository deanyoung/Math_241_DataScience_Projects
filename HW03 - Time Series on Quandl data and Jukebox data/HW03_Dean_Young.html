The estimate for rho is above 1. After adjusting the standard error for serial correlation with HAC standard errors, we find that 1 is not in the 95% confidence interval for rho. This means that aapl is non-stationary over time meaning that it should not regress to a mean and should be continually growing. Also, random shocks should move the stock to a new trend and not return to any old ones. However, rho is extremely close to 1 which means aapl shouldnt just be blowing up but rather similar to a random walk model where rho=1. This suggested pattern is reflected in the graph of aapls price over time. Dont believe all those stock analysts that say they can predict aapls movements.
